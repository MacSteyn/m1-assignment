Happy Paws Pet Adoption

Happy Paws Pet Adoption is a local community service dedicated to rescuing abandoned and homeless pets and finding them loving homes.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote animal welfare, responsible pet ownership, and reduce the number of homeless animals in our community.

Why Adopt?

  • You save a life
  • You help reduce animal homelessness
  • Adopted pets are loving and loyal
